Party Mode is an experimental audio visualizer powered by d3.js and the web audio api. Dragging audio files into the browser should work. It requires a modern browser and a decent CPU. This app was developed by Mathew Preziotte with help and inspiration from:

  • Javascript -- Mike Bostock's, bl.ocks: #7782500, #3795048, #4248145, #7782500, Stack Overflow, Code Theory, Mousetrap.js, jQuery, and DevelopPHP. Michael Gerhaeuser, Los Techies
  • Design -- Codrops [1] [2] ColourLovers [1] [2] [3] [4] [5], aronwoost, Dustin Cartwright, Ceaser , Headphones by Kevin Hipke and Record by Juan Pablo Bravo from The Noun Project icomoon, iconmelon, fontello, iconmonstr.
